  • Myers Briggs Personality Types

The Myers Briggs Test is based on the theory proposed by Carl Jung which stated that there are four cognitive functions (thinking, feeling, sensation, and intuition) whose combination define sixteen different personalities. The Big Five test is based on the theory that decribes human personality based on five fators that have been defined as openness to experience, conscientiousness, extraversion, agreeableness, and neuroticism.All the questions in the test is compulsory to know the accurate result.
